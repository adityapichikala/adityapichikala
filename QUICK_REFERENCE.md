## üé® Theme Options

## ‚ö° Quick Customization

### Change Colors:
- `title_color=6366F1` - Purple
- `icon_color=6366F1` - Purple
- `text_color=E2E8F0` - Light gray
- `bg_color=0D1117` - Dark background

### Hide Elements:
- `&hide=stars,commits,prs,issues`
- `&hide_border=true`
- `&hide_rank=true`

### Show Private Contributions:
- `&count_private=true`
- `&include_all_commits=true`
